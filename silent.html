Silent Era

“In the time of silent film there used to be a lot of good pictures. Now that’s all come to an end.”



The silent era was popular by many individuals. They never spoke dialogue in the movie. They would show cards called title cards for people to understand what that situtaion is about. Also many of the movies in the silent era were 10-12 minutes long.

Calling the movies silent films is a mistake because many movie theaters provided sounds. However, some films were produced with complete musical scores. Most early silent films were accompanied with a musical background to feature the narrative on the screen. Some even had live actors or narrators.

Unfortunately, many of the early classics have been lost to decomposing film bases and outright destruction. Many early silent films were either dramas, epics, romances, or comedies. This era was movies that didnt have adjusted sound, from the earliest film around 1891 until it ended in 1929.
